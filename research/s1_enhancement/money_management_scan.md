## Working interpretation
- Sizing does not change hit-rate; it changes path risk and yen P&L. Winrate changes only if the trade filter changes.
- Drawdown-after boost is intentionally included as a hypothesis test; treat it as dangerous unless it improves both full and test MAR with acceptable min equity.
- Sticky new-high / equity-step variants are the candidates for 'when to size up after profits'.
